What is VOCALOID

───── ⋆⋅☆⋅⋆ ─────


VOCALOID is a singing voice synthesizer software developed by Yamaha Corporation. It allows users to create songs by inputting lyrics and melody, which the software then synthesizes into a singing voice using pre-recorded voice samples from various voice actors and singers. VOCALOID has gained significant popularity, especially in Japan, with virtual idols like Hatsune Miku becoming cultural phenomena.

This software is NOT FREE, you have to buy it. But if you are like me, then you can always pirate it from El Kistune to enjoy it for free. (Although it is quite unethical to do so)


Notable VOCALOID characters include Hatsune Miku, Kagamine Rin and Len, Megurine Luka, KAITO, and MEIKO. Each character has its own unique voice.

VOCALOID is widely used in music production, live performances, and various multimedia projects, allowing creators to produce music without the need for a human singer.

It has a large and active community of users and fans who create and share their own music, artwork, and videos featuring VOCALOID characters.

The software has also been used in various commercial applications.
