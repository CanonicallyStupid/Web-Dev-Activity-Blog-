What is Synthesizer V

───── ⋆⋅☆⋅⋆ ─────


Synthesizer V is a singing voice synthesizer developed by Dreamtonics. It allows users to create realistic singing by inputting lyrics and melody, which the software then synthesizes into a singing voice using advanced AI technology. Synthesizer V has gained popularity for its high-quality voice banks and user-friendly interface.
