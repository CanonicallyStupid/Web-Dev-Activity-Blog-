What is MMD

───── ⋆⋅☆⋅⋆ ─────


MikuMikuDance (MMD) is a free 3D animation software originally developed for creating dance animations featuring the virtual idol Hatsune Miku. It is a game, without the game. It allows users to create and animate 3D models, primarily of anime-style characters, by manipulating their movements and poses. MMD has gained a large following among fans of Vocaloid and 3D animation, leading to a vibrant community that shares models, motion data, and tutorials.

This software is FREE to download and use. You can download it from Learn MMD


MMD is often used to create music videos, dance animations, and other creative projects. The software supports various file formats for 3D models, motion data, and accessories, allowing users to customize their animations extensively. MMD has become a popular tool for both amateur and professional animators due to its accessibility and the extensive resources available within its community.


One of the most popular and useful sites to download MMD models is DeviantArt. You can find many MMD models, stages, effects, motions, textures, skyboxes, etc. there, both free and some are paid.
